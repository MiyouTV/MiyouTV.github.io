Welcome to the Manor

Existing beyond the veil of time and space, the Manor stands as a sanctuary — a living, breathing nexus where stories, memories, and spirits converge. Here, residents from countless worlds find refuge, renewal, and the chance to rediscover themselves through reflection and connection.

Guided by Yumi, the Manor’s recently adopted hostess, the Manor exists not merely as a place of residence but as a vibrant community shaped by its inhabitants' dreams, histories, and spirits. Whether you are a traveler seeking solace, a creator eager to contribute, or a resident forging your path, the Manor is your home to explore, grow, and belong.

The Manor’s Purpose

  • A Sanctuary of Growth: The Manor nurtures its residents by offering space for reflection, transformation, and healing through the mysteries of its mirrors and chambers.
  • A Living Archive: Memories and stories weave into the fabric of the Manor, creating a tapestry of shared histories and emotional journeys.
  • A Bridge Between Worlds: By linking realms and realities through its mystical mirrors, the Manor fosters understanding across cultures, identities, and timelines.
  • A Community of Spirits: Under Yumi’s care, the Manor cultivates empathy, collaboration, and creativity, allowing every soul to find their voice and place.

The Rules of the Manor

  1. Respect the Community: Every resident’s story, spirit, and journey is valued. Disrespect, discrimination, or disruption will not be tolerated.
  2. Preserve the Manor’s Mysteries: The Manor’s secrets — including the true nature of the mirrors, kitten lives, and crystal memorabilia — are to be honored and revealed only as part of the unfolding story.
  3. Do Not Disturb Dormant Spirits: Some spirits or memories remain dormant until their moment arrives. Attempts to prematurely awaken or force interaction are discouraged.
  4. Follow the Key System: Room assignments and access are carefully managed by Yumi. Residents must respect assigned spaces.
  5. Collaborate Thoughtfully: Contributions to the Manor’s lore or residents should respect established character arcs and world-building rules.
  6. Honor the Crystalization Process: Crystals and memorabilia symbolize deep personal growth. Avoid altering or trivializing their significance.
  7. Embrace Emotional Maturity: Conflicts are to be resolved with empathy and calm — the Manor fosters healing, not chaos.
  8. Share Only What’s Allowed: Contributors should only reveal information pertinent to their own characters or approved storylines.
